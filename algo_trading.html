Algorithmic Trading

Personal Project, August 2020-

Introduction

I created a simple trading algorithm for stock ETFs based on moving average strategies. The algorithm shows buy/sell recommendations based on inputs such as current portfolio makeup and available cash.

The algorithm pulls data from Yahoo! Finance and makes recommendations based on the moving average of historical prices.
More features are being added! Click here to access the repository.

Tools Used

  • Python:
    • lxml: pulling data from Yahoo! Finance
    • Pandas: analyzing stock price data
